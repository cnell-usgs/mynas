population estimates of COMY

Analyses of bird populations on the island of Tutuila, American Samoa.

In 2013 point-transect surveys were conducted along urban-natural gradients, in observation of the local bird community. Abundance counts were recorded with special emphasis to the presence of Common Mynas, Acridotheres tristis, a hyper-abundant exotic bird species that has plagued American Samoa as well as many other South Pacific islands. Analyses here are directed at estimating the island-wide population of Common Mynas, and environmental predictors of Myna density.
